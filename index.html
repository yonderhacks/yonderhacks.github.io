A Digital Adventure

Our approach to redefining the digital making experience at the local scene involves exploring the amazing capabilities of inexpensive maker tools and services through hands-on learning engagements and activities, leveraging free and accessible digital resources made by/for makers worldwide. Through this drive, we hope to model an open and inclusive environment void of bottlenecks, inspiring a new generation of hobbyists, tinkerers, hackers and inventors to learn collaboratively and support each other in creating value for the maker community and the world in general.
